About Us

Discover our restaurant story

Since 1976, Mamta Bhojnalaya has been a cherished spot for homely Indian vegetarian meals. Started as a small eatery by a loving mother, it quickly became a favorite for its authentic flavors and warm hospitality. Generations have savored its simple yet soulful dishes, prepared with traditional recipes and fresh ingredients. Over the years, it has grown, but its essence of love and purity remains unchanged. Even today, every meal served carries the taste of tradition and the warmth of home.
